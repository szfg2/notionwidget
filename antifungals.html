Scope and assumptions: “Coverage” means typical clinical activity, not guaranteed susceptibility. Always check species, MIC, site penetration, host immune status, local resistance, and drug interactions. Echinocandins have limited urinary and CNS penetration. Azoles have clinically irritating CYP interactions and usually need TDM for itraconazole, voriconazole, and posaconazole. Amphotericin B covers broadly but exact formulation and toxicity matter.
