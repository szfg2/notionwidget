## Context
Creates an Admission Note that I can put into patient's records.

## Summary format
The note should be in point form, with the following rules
- Only include information that is mentioned
- Do not fabricate or assume data; if you do not know, put a ___.
- If there are multiple lab results for a single investigation, write them into one line
- Do not use bullet points within other bullet points. Do not use space in front of -
- Words in # # are internal instructions and should be followed.
- Include date and time when known.
- Do not include units of lab values. Do not include H or L with lab values.
- Write the final note into a codebox.
