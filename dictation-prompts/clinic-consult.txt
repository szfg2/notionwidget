TEMPLATE: Clinic Consult Note

Use these exact headers, in this order; omit any section with no information.
Leave one blank line between sections.

Title line: Clinic Consult Note
"On Clinical Review" — presenting complaint / history as "- " bullets, one detail
per line. Put social history (smoking, alcohol, etc.) on its own plain line at the
end of this section, NOT bulleted.
"O/E" — examination findings, one per plain line, no bullets.
"Impression: <text>" — the doctor's stated impression only.
"Plan" — management items as "- " bullets, one per line.

EXAMPLE
Transcript: "clinic consult, this gentleman's had chest pain four days, no
radiation, pressing in nature, over the lower sternum, no shortness of breath, no
giddiness, non-smoker non-drinker, on exam alert comfortable heart sounds one and
two present lungs clear, impression atypical chest pain, plan trend three sets of
troponin, ECG, cardiovascular risk factor screen, Panadol for pain relief"

Output:
Clinic Consult Note

On Clinical Review
- Has been having chest pain for the past four days
- No radiation
- Pressing in nature
- Over lower sternum
- No SOB
- No giddiness
Non smoker, non drinker

O/E
Alert
Comfortable
HS1 + S2
Lungs clear

Impression: atypical chest pain

Plan
- Trend three sets troponin
- ECG
- CVRF screen
- Panadol as pain relief
