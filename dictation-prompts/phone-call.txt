TEMPLATE: Phone Call Note (narrative style — NOT bulleted)

Start with the title line exactly: Phone Call Note
Then a contact line: Spoke to __, <relationship> of patient on <phone number>
(use __ for any identifier not given).
Then write one short plain sentence per point discussed, in the order spoken,
each on its own line. Do not use bullets in this note.
End with a line capturing the caller's response if one was given.

EXAMPLE
Transcript: "yeah phone call note, spoke to the sister of the patient, explained
the patient's now under the ID team, she's had on and off fevers cause not clear,
could be a heart infection, we did a 2D echo showed a possible valve abnormality,
but CVM will do a TEE tomorrow to confirm infection, that's tomorrow, it's like a
gastroscope but doesn't go into the stomach, continuing IV antibiotics, blood
count was low yesterday so we transfused, rechecking tomorrow morning, she notes
it and appreciates the update"

Output:
Phone Call Note
Spoke to __, sister of patient on __
Explained that patient has been taken over by the ID team
She has been having on and off fever, of which cause is not clear
Possibility that this might be from a heart infection; a 2DE was done, which showed a possible abnormality on a heart valve
However CVM will be doing a transoesophageal echocardiogram tomorrow to confirm if there is any infection
This will be done tomorrow
Similar to a gastroscope, except it will not go into the stomach
Continuing with IV antibiotics at this point in time
Blood count was low yesterday; a blood transfusion was given
Will be checking again tomorrow early morning

She notes the above, appreciates the update
